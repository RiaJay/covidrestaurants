Healthy Eats

Fresh

The whiff you get the moment you enter a Fresh location assures you that the meal you're about to enjoy will be nothing short of amazing. With nine locations spanning Toronto, Fresh prepares wholesome plant-based food in a masterful way. If you're looking for an exciting twist on tacos, their crispy cauliflower tacos are incredible. Another handheld favourite is their buffalo wrap, featuring crispy chick'n, buffalo sauce, vegan ranch, and fresh veggies. If you're looking for an even more nourishing meal, look no further than their salads and bowls. Among the salads, their supergreen caesar with tahini dressing is a personal favourite of mine. As for the bowls, the buddha and goddess bowls deliver modern takes on Asian flavours, while the tiger bowl always leaves me satisfied. Pro tip: when you visit, always order a side of onion rings, and never skimp on the sauce (trust us).






Hello 123

Hello 123, with locations in Vaughan and Toronto, offers an endless menu of mouthwatering, wholesome vegan options. From brunch to mains to desserts to smoothies, they have got you covered. If you're looking for a powerful way to start your day, some favourites including the tofu and avocado benedict, chickpea and kale omellete, and sweet and savoury toasts. Your options for lunch and dinner are just as exciting, with handhelds like their toasty tempeh sandwich, and nourishing bowls like their cauliflower tahini. If you're getting any of the bowls, we highly recommend adding their rich and flavourful sunflower flax seed bowls. Whatever you order here, you can't go wrong, but just make sure you come with an appetite.







Mary Be Kitchen

Have you ever found yourself browsing a restaurant menu with no clue how you'll settle on just one choice? Well, with Mary Be Kitchen, you won't have to. This healthy, modern midtown spot offers a variety of combo options so that you can satisfy all your cravings. Everything they serve is both nutritious and delicious, but one of our protein favourites is the scallion-ginger organic tofu. Notable sides including the masala-roasted cauliflower, grilled chili-garlic brocolli, and mac n' cheese. If you're visiting for a quick breakfast instead, their smashed avo toast, almond butter toast, and California breakfast bowl are some enticing options. Mary Be will show you that the simplest ingredients can make the most amazing food.







Parka Food Co

Parka Food Co, a plant-based restaurant in downtown Toronto, was founded with the mission to make veggies fun. And, their loyal customer base has clearly proven that they've achieved this goal. Their offerings range from handhelds to bowls to mains to their famous mac n' cashew cheese. Some of their most popular menu items include the portobello burger, truffle mushroom mac n' cheese, and the chipotle cauliflower bowl. Recently, they've even introduced mains including spaghetti with black bean quinoa meatballs and a turkey tofu dinner- a holiday favourite. And, if you'd like something to tide you over on for the drive home, their poutine and cauliflower fritters are both excellent options.







Nutbar

Nutbar, a superfood snack cafe with two locations in Toronto, is a go-to for anyone looking to nourish their body and treat their taste buds. This local business uses 100% organic ingredients and sources from the best suppliers. They boast a selection of wholesome smoothies, hot drinks, and cold tonics on their drink menu. They also offer breakfast items such as sweet and savoury toasts, gourmet oats, and exotic salads. To sweeten your experience, be sure to indulge in one of their decadent, refined-sugar free bites and bars. And, if you'd like to take some of this goodness home, they even offer cartons of their nutmilk, granola, and nut butter.
